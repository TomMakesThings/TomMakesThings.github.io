scRNA-seq Differential Expression Benchmarking (2021)

Working in collaboration with UK DIR Imperial College London, I developed a pipeline to benchmark pseudobulk, pseudoreplicate and mixture model methods to identify differentially expressed genes in microglia.

This parallelizable pipeline was created from scratch and runs the following R packages: DESeq2, edgeR, limma, Welch and Wilcoxon t-tests, MAST and NEBULA. Multiple interactive graphs are produced to compare the methods against a ground truth and to identify up and down regulated genes.

Clustering and Topological Data Analysis of Single-Cell RNA Sequencing Data (2020 - 2021)

Using Python and R, I experimented with various dimensionality reduction techniques including autoencoders, clustering methods and the Mapper algorithm to identify sub-populations in real and simulated scRNA-seq datasets. For one dataset, I assigned cells to the correct cell line with 99.9% accuracy, a result significantly higher than the original research paper.

Report: Clustering and Topological Data Analysis of Single-Cell RNA Sequencing Data
