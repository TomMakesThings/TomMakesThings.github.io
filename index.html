PLASMID (2021)

Working in collaboration with UK DIR Imperial College London, I developed a pipeline to benchmark pseudobulk, pseudoreplicate and mixture model methods to identify differentially expressed genes in microglia.

This parallelizable pipeline was created from scratch and runs the following R packages: DESeq2, edgeR, limma, Welch and Wilcoxon t-tests, MAST and NEBULA. Multiple interactive graphs are produced to compare the methods against a ground truth and to identify up and down regulated genes.

IMDb Movie Genre Predictor (2021)
